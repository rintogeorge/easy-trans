Overview

Easy Trans is a transportation company based in Bangalore, with a decade of experience specializing in local transportation of loose cargo and container movement. Over the past 10 years, Easy Trans has built a reliable service network that ensures efficient and timely delivery of cargo both within Bangalore and across other parts of the country.

The company has been engaged in the port movements to major ports for two years. Easy Trans’ team is specialized in handling temperature sensitive cargos (Reefer containers). Assurance of quality and timely service is empowered.

Experience

With a decade-long presence in the industry, Easy Trans has developed expertise in both local and interstate transportation.
